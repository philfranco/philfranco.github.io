Phil's Services

Below are a select few examples of past projects I've undertaken. Should you have any concepts or inquiries regarding drone services, I eagerly await your correspondence. Please click here to get in touch!

Business Tour

I was commissioned to capture footage of a plant nursery and Techo-Bloc supplier. I navigated my drone through the entirety of the campus, even venturing through the garden center.

Warehouse Progress

A one of a kind, unique perspective to advertising the progress on the construction of a warehouse. I conduct monthly visits to capture video footage illustrating the evolving stages of development.

Wedding "One Take"

Witness my meticulous choreography of the groomsmen in an immersive video, showcasing the venue's exquisite beauty from every angle, both inside and out.

College Tours

Need a way to show prospect students the campus in an innovative and exciting way? Look no further. Witness as we soar into TCNJ's cutting-edge STEM building, offering a comprehensive glimpse into the campus atmosphere and its amenities.

Music Video

Drones elevate the filming experience with their ability to capture excitement and energy, particularly showcased in music videos. Within the Best of 2023 compilation, viewers will find an exclusive preview of footage intended for an upcoming music video by a promising artist.

Action Shots

Vehicles such as drift cars and dirt bikes are ideally suited for drone cinematography. FPV drones offer viewers a immersive experience similar to that of a video game, placing them at the center of the action.
