Phil's Experience

Welcome to Phil's Drone Services! I'm excited to share my passion for drones and aerial photography with you. As a drone enthusiast with over four years of experience, I've witnessed the incredible evolution of drone technology firsthand. Drones have revolutionized industries ranging from filmmaking to agriculture, and I'm proud to be a part of this exciting journey. I currenlty reside in the greater Philadelphia Region, but am excited for travel opportunities!

Flying Skills

With countless hours logged in the sky, I've honed my flying skills to perfection. Whether navigating tight spaces or capturing sweeping vistas, I pride myself on executing smooth and precise drone flights that deliver stunning results. I also have multiple certifications such as a FAA Part 107 Drone Pilots License.

The Drone Fleet

I own and operate a diverse fleet of drones suited for various tasks. From compact drones for indoor filming around people to robust models for outdoor exploration, each drone in my arsenal is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure optimal performance.

Custom Drone Building Expertise

One of my specialties is building custom drones tailored to specific job requirements. With a keen understanding of both drone technology and client needs, I design and assemble drones optimized for a wide range of different shots.
